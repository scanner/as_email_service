{{ settings.SITE_NAME }} is a self-hosted email service that lets you send and receive email through your own domain using a provider of your choice.

Features

  • Send email via SMTP from your own domain
  • Send and receive email via Postmark or ForwardEmail.net
  • Deliver incoming mail to your IMAP server of choice
  • Automatic spam classification via SpamAssassin with Bayesian filter training
  • Alias addresses per email account
  • Multiple delivery methods per account
