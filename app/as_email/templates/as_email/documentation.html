Your Email Accounts

You may have one or more email accounts on this server, each with its own email address. All of your email accounts are listed on the main page after you log in.

Each email account has its own password used to authenticate for both sending (SMTP) and receiving (IMAP) on this server. This password is separate from your login password for this web interface. Set or change an email account's password from its card on the main page.

Sending Email

Configure your email client with one set of SMTP credentials per email account. Use the email address and its account password (not your web login password).

Server{{ settings.SITE_NAME }}
Port587 (STARTTLS)
UsernameThe email account address (e.g. you@{{ settings.SITE_NAME }})
PasswordThe email account password (set on the main page)
AuthenticationRequired

Local Delivery

Local delivery stores incoming mail in directories on this server. This is the simplest delivery method and requires no external accounts or services.

This server also runs an IMAP service that provides access to those local mail directories. To read your email directly from this service, configure your email client to connect via IMAP using your email account's address and password:

Server{{ settings.SITE_NAME }}
Port993 (SSL)
UsernameThe email account address
PasswordThe email account password (set on the main page)

IMAP Delivery

IMAP delivery forwards incoming mail received by this service to a third-party IMAP server of your choice (e.g. Gmail, Fastmail, or your own hosted server). Each message is deposited via the IMAP protocol into the inbox of that remote server. If spam auto-filing is enabled, spam is filed into the junk folder as designated by the remote IMAP service. Configure IMAP delivery per email account on the main page.

  • Set the remote IMAP server, port, username, and password
  • Normal mail is delivered to the inbox
  • Spam (when auto-filing is enabled) is delivered to the remote junk folder

Alias Delivery

Alias delivery lets you receive email sent to multiple addresses and have it all delivered to a single email account. For example, if you have a primary account alice@{{ settings.SITE_NAME }} and a second account info@{{ settings.SITE_NAME }}, you can set the second account to alias to the first — so mail sent to either address arrives in Alice's inbox.

This is the recommended way to consolidate several email addresses without managing separate inboxes for each one.

Spam Filtering

Incoming mail is automatically scanned by SpamAssassin. You can configure how spam is handled per email account:

  • Do nothing — deliver as normal regardless of spam score
  • Auto-file — move spam to a designated spam folder

SpamAssassin uses a Bayesian filter that can be trained over time to improve accuracy for your specific mail patterns.
