About Delivery Methods:

Local Delivery: Delivers email to a local folder with optional spam filtering. All email accounts start with a local delivery method by default.

Alias-To: Forwards email to another one of your email accounts. Select from your available accounts in the dropdown.

IMAP Delivery: Delivers email to an external IMAP account with username/password or OAuth authentication. For Gmail, use the “Connect to Gmail” button to authenticate via OAuth.

When email is received, it will be delivered to all enabled delivery methods for that account. Every account must have at least one delivery method.
